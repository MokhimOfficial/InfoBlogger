Domino's Pizza

  • Domino's is a global pizza chain famous for its delivery and carryout options. They offer a wide variety of pizzas with vegetarian and non-vegetarian options.

  • You can also find sides like chicken wings, pasta, and beverages on their menu. Domino's is known for their crust options, including hand-tossed, thin crust, and even cheesy Paratha crust (in India).

  • They have a loyalty program that rewards frequent customers with points redeemable for future purchases. Domino's recently revamped their loyalty program to offer more benefits and attract new customers.

  • You can order online or through their app for delivery or carryout. They also partnered with Uber Eats for additional delivery options.

  • Domino's has over 18,000 stores worldwide. Their phone number in India is 1800-208-1234 for placing orders or contacting customer service.
