Engineer Wadewale

  • Engineer Wadewale is a popular fast-food restaurant chain in Pune, Maharashtra, India.They specialize in Maharashtrian snacks like Misal Pav and Vada Pav.

  • They also offer other options like sandwiches and mini meals.Customer reviews indicate they have good service and tasty food.

  • There are several locations across Pune, including Thergaon, Baner, and Kothrud.Their hours vary slightly by location, but most are open early mornings to late evenings.
